Summer Film

With an extreme love for summer, and a newfound love for the indescribable look of film photos, I wanted to combine the two into an app I could use to transform any of my iPhone photos into nostalgic but classic photos with that film feel.

iOS • Design • Development

Head Gardener

Head Gardener puts a personal Master Gardener in your back pocket. Gardeners know the importance of walking their gardens and taking notes of what they see, changes that are happening, and issues they need to fix. Head Gardener allows you to voice brain dump everything you see in your garden, and it creates a detailed maintenance plan and answers all the questions you have. Head Gardener is your companion to your best garden yet.

iOS • AI • Design

Florals AI

Florals AI was created so users can not only identify a flower type by snapping a photo, but know the exact variety type the flower is so they can replicate the exact flower and color of that flower in their own garden. A loved feature is the ability to upload an image of a favorite bouquet, and the app breaks down each flower and variety type that makes up the bouquet. It makes growing your dream bouquet so easy.

iOS • AI • Design
